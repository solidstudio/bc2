Thinking Ahead

Planning & awareness of strategy

Planning for the future relies on setting and developing strategies, then working towards them to realise organisational goals. It’s identifying and building a positive and compelling vision of tomorrow’s Barclaycard at the same time as being realistic about the issues faced today. And demonstrating financial awareness while recognising important business opportunities.

Situation / Task

  • Company strategy to diversify & expand bunsiness beyond automotive sector
  • Double business in 2 years from 2012
  • Introduce new products to the market to complement existing solutions and compete with competition
  • Increase modular based solutions and code re-use to save money and time
  • Grow and develop existing accounts
  • Rebrand and re-align company vision and values
  • Update legacy systems for future proof alternatives
  • Reduce maintenance and setup times
  • Set solid foundations to build upon
  • Identify missing roles and processes needed to achieve business goals

Actions

  • Structured, built, and documented new project templates for existing and future projects
  • Actively supported pitches and delivered new business for clients outside the automotive industry
  • Development of existing accounts within GM, VW Group and PSA
  • Successfully utilized employee recognition platform from BI Worldwide US
  • Built re-usable modules & systems including games, mobile sites, charting frameworks, site resources and assets
  • Design and build for new company intranet
  • Extensive research and evaluation of current & future technologies
  • Replaced legacy solutions with future proof alternatives
  • Helped define and agree standards and approaches to codebase
  • Designed and built core frontend architecture to standardise and build upon
  • Sourced and trained additional resource where necessary

Results

  • Won and delivered contracts for a number of non-automotive clients including Vaillant, Blackberry, Dell, Johnson & Johnson, CAT and Neilson
  • Increased size and spend of GM, VW Group and PSA accounts
  • Company ahead of target to double size of business in 2 years
  • More stable future proof codebase
  • Increased team size, knowledge and synergy
  • Reduced turn around times for new projects and project maintenance
  • Quicker to add new resource with less overhead
  • New case studies and products to help gain future opportunities
  • Significant financial savings and gains
