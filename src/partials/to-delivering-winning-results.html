Taking Ownership

Delivering winning results

Delivering winning results means consistently achieving project and work goals. It’s being able to plan activities well in advance, identifying and organising resources needed to accomplish tasks, and accounting for possible obstacles along the way. It’s all about control too – controlling costs by thinking in terms of profit, loss and added value, plus managing expectations effectively. In addition to understanding and complying with a control environment, delivering on promises rests on the ability to monitor performance against deadlines and milestones.

Situation / Task

  • Need to maintain contract with PSA Finance
  • Multiple audiences to accomodate
  • Particularly demanding customer
  • High value account with contract renewed every 3 years
  • Need to maintain very creative and interactive solution
  • High past standards set

Actions

  • Recommended built and implemented highly interactive and engaging solutions
  • Sourced and implemented mobile optimised application
  • Provided multiple options in accordance with customer demands
  • Participated in brainstorms with designers, developers and stakeholders
  • Utilised project plans incorporating deadlines and milestones to ensure project kept on track

Results

  • Extremely high customer satisfaction with 9.0 csi scores
  • Project delivered on time and within budget
  • Client business retained
  • Increased customer engagment, satisfaction, performance, sales and profits
  • Increased traffic outside core business hours due to mobile application
  • Successfully developed new efficient ways to meet demands of this long term customer
