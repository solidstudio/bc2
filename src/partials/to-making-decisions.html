Taking Ownership

Making decisions

Making clear and definite decisions within appropriate timescales is vital. Sometimes these decisions may involve making tough choices, plus taking measured risks – so consideration is equally as important as confidence. It’s about gathering comprehensive data to support responsible decision-making, as well as drawing on past successes and failures to inform decisions.

Situation / Task

  • Existing important client (Audi) looking for development of new web application in addition to existing programme
  • Client undergoing brand refresh resulting in limited infomation to work with
  • New content management system
  • Lot at stake as existing business under threat
  • Programme needed to compliment clients ambition to become number one luxury car brand

Actions

  • Carried out extensive brand research to gain understanding of customer direction
  • Had to make informed decisions based on research results and take measured risks
  • Had to work within very short timelines for decision making and project completion

Results

  • Engagement up within dealership
  • Sales increased
  • Helped Audi to keep on track for company objective to become no. 1 luxury car manufacturer
  • Recognised by Audi as a key partner
  • Secured future business
  • New clients as a result - VW, Bentley
